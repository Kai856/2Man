About DegreeMap

At DegreeMap, we believe that the path to graduation should be clear and achievable. Our mission is to help students visually track their academic progress and get personalized guidance for their unique academic journeys. Whether you're a freshman just getting started or a senior planning your last steps, DegreeMap keeps you on course for success. Let us help you navigate your journey toward graduation with ease and confidence.

Founded by students for students, DegreeMap is built to simplify the process of managing academic requirements. We understand the challenges students face when it comes to tracking courses, majors, and university requirements. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly tool that alleviates the stress of managing your academic roadmap. The future is bright, and we're here to help you reach it, one step at a time.
